AI Insights AI 洞察

01

Agentic Search Is Becoming Infrastructure

Nebius's $275–400 M acquisition of Tavily signals that real-time web search is an expected primitive in any AI cloud stack. Standalone search API vendors without a broader platform will face margin pressure as hyperscalers bundle search natively — pricing power will erode unless differentiated by quality or latency.

智能体搜索正成为基础设施

Nebius 以 2.75 亿至 4 亿美元收购 Tavily，表明实时网页搜索已成为 AI 云平台的基础能力。随着超大规模云厂商将搜索原生集成，缺乏完整平台的独立搜索 API 厂商将面临定价压力。

02

Scrapers Are Evolving Into Research Agents

Firecrawl's Spark model family, Exa's three-tier search (Fast / Auto / Deep), and Oxylabs' self-healing parsers all point to the same shift: the market is moving from raw-HTML extraction toward intelligent, outcome-oriented retrieval. Products delivering only unstructured content will commoditize under margin pressure from agent-native competitors.

爬虫正演化为研究智能体

Firecrawl 的 Spark 模型系列、Exa 的三级搜索体系（快速/自动/深度）以及 Oxylabs 的自愈解析器，均指向同一趋势：市场正从原始 HTML 抓取转向智能、结果导向的检索。仅提供非结构化内容的产品将在智能体原生竞争者的冲击下商品化。

03

Open-Source Carries Real Enterprise Security Risk

Crawl4AI's v0.8.6 litellm supply-chain compromise is a textbook example. As procurement teams evaluate self-hosted crawlers against SaaS alternatives, CVE history and dependency hygiene will become formal selection criteria — creating a window for vendors with strong security posture to win deals they previously lost on price.

开源方案存在真实的企业安全风险

Crawl4AI v0.8.6 的 litellm 供应链入侵是典型案例。随着采购团队将自托管爬虫与 SaaS 方案进行比较，CVE 历史和依赖健康度将成为正式选型标准，为具备强安全态势的厂商创造差异化优势。
