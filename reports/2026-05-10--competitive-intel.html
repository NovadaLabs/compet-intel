03

Firecrawl's Spark Models Create Lock-In — Publish Novada's Model-Agnostic Position

Firecrawl's Spark 1 model family (Fast/Mini/Pro) powers its /agent endpoint — technically impressive, but it means developers are now dependent on Firecrawl's model roadmap, pricing, and availability. novada-search's architecture (search + proxy, no proprietary model layer) means developers keep full model choice — Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini, or their own. This is model portability: a genuine advantage for enterprise teams with model governance requirements or anyone benchmarking across models. Without an explicit statement, developers will read the absence of Novada models as a gap, not a design choice. Silence on this cedes an architectural strength.

Firecrawl Spark 模型创造锁定——发布 Novada 模型无关性立场

Firecrawl 的 Spark 1 系列驱动 /agent 端点，但开发者现在依赖于 Firecrawl 的模型路线图。novada-search 架构意味着开发者保留完整模型选择。这是模型可移植性：对企业团队的真正优势。没有明确声明，开发者会将 Novada 缺少模型视为差距而非设计选择。
