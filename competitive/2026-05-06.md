## AI Insights

1. **Exa's breaking deprecations are today's biggest integration risk.** The May 1 deadline has now passed — `use_autoprompt`, `highlights`, and Auto relevance scores are gone from production. Any Novada customer or internal system that calls Exa without migrating will receive degraded or broken responses silently (fields simply missing from JSON). A one-time audit sweep against the official May 2026 changelog should be prioritized before any new Exa integrations ship. The pattern is notable: Exa is aggressively pruning the API surface to streamline toward its Exa 2.0 architecture, trading short-term compatibility pain for a cleaner long-term interface.

2. **BrightData's 100 % MCP benchmark score is an enterprise sales asset that its competitors cannot easily counter.** A third-party 250-agent load test showing 100 % success rate is the kind of vendor-neutral proof point that closes procurement reviews. Oxylabs, Decodo, and Firecrawl each have strong individual product stories, but none has a comparable published benchmark for MCP-based concurrent agent workloads. If Novada operates or plans to operate in the MCP-agent infrastructure layer, this benchmark sets the floor for what enterprise buyers will expect in RFP responses — Novada should be able to articulate its own concurrency and reliability story in quantified terms.

3. **Apify's Shopify competitive-intelligence demo is a direct product-positioning threat.** Demonstrating market activation, competitor research, and price monitoring in a single workflow is the same value proposition Novada anchors its go-to-market on. Apify has the distribution advantage of 80+ MCP servers and a large existing developer community, while its pricing and Actor marketplace lower the barrier to assembling such pipelines. The differentiation question for Novada is whether depth (domain-specific intelligence, curated data, analytical synthesis) beats breadth (Apify's general-purpose scraping platform with hundreds of pre-built Actors). The window to establish a clearer category position before Apify's May 5 demo drives buyer awareness is closing.
